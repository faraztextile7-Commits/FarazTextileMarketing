1- Cotton Yarn

Cotton yarn is a soft, breathable, and natural fiber yarn made from cotton plants. It is comfortable, skin-friendly, and highly absorbent, making it ideal for clothing, towels, and home textiles.

Key Features:

  • Soft and comfortable for everyday wear.
  • Breathable and moisture-absorbing.
  • Durable and long-lasting.
  • Eco-friendly and natural.

Uses:

home textiles (bedsheets, towels), and accessories (scarves, hats).

2- Wool Yarn

Wool yarn is a natural fiber yarn from sheep or animal fleece, known for its warmth, elasticity, and softness. It is perfect for sweaters, scarves, blankets, and winter apparel.

Key Features:

  • Provides excellent insulation and warmth.
  • Soft, elastic, and durable.
  • Naturally breathable and moisture-wicking.
  • Ideal for cold-weather textiles.

Uses:

Sweaters, scarves, socks, blankets, and premium winter garments.

3- Silk Yarn

Luxurious natural yarn from silkworms, smooth, shiny, and soft. Ideal for fine garments and premium textiles.

Key Features:

  • Lustrous, lightweight, strong, and elegant.

4- Polyester Yarn

Durable synthetic yarn, strong, wrinkle-resistant, and versatile. Perfect for clothing, home textiles, and industrial fabrics.

Key Features:

  • Long-lasting, low-maintenance, and colorfast.
